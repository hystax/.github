# Hystax – FinOps and MLOps solution provider 

[Hystax](https://hystax.com) is a leading provider of FinOps and MLOps solutions, helping companies achieve full cost transparency and efficiency in the cloud while enabling optimal performance of ML workloads.
Our flagship products – OptScale for FinOps and Kiroframe for MLOps – empower organizations to control infrastructure costs, run ML/AI workloads efficiently, and accelerate innovation.


## Hystax mission 

Our mission is to help companies optimize the performance and cost of ML model training jobs, as well as increase the number of experiments an ML engineer can run.

Hystax is currently the solution of choice for iconic brands such as PwC, Ives Rocher, Nokia, DHL, and Airbus, due to its FinOps/MLOps adoption, which offers them a platform that promotes complete cloud cost visibility and numerous optimization recommendations.


## Hystax flagship solutions

### OptScale – FinOps Platform

OptScale is Hystax’s flagship FinOps product, providing complete transparency into cloud usage and delivering actionable optimization scenarios. Access to the OptScale open-source solution is granted to users under the Apache 2.0 license.

Key capabilities include:
-  Cloud cost visibility and analytics across teams
-  Dozens of optimization scenarios: rightsizing, Reserved/Spot instances, Savings Plans, etc.
-  Policy-based governance and budget control
-  Assignment rules and workload prioritization.

OptScale is available for on-premise deployment and as SaaS at [https://my.optscale.com](https://my.optscale.com).

As part of Hystax’s involvement in the Linux Foundation, OptScale continues to evolve in line with open, community-driven FinOps practices.

### Kiroframe – MLOps Platform

Kiroframe is Hystax’s dedicated MLOps solution designed to optimize ML/AI workloads and accelerate experimentation.

Key capabilities include:

-  ML/AI task profiling and metrics transparency
-  Performance optimization with detailed bottleneck analysis and recommendations
-  Automated experiment management with budget-aware runsets
-  Support for various hardware, datasets, and hyperparameter tuning.

Kiroframe supports both on-premise installation and SaaS deployment, accessible at [https://my.kiroframe.com](https://my.kiroframe.com).
